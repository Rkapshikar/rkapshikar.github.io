About Me

I'm a fourth-year student at UC Berkeley studying EECS and economics. My work experience includes engineering internships at Microsoft and Google and a product management internship at Workday. I also contribute to datajournalism projects for the Daily Californian, and manage a team of talented developers and designers as the Chief Technology Officer for the ASUC, Berkeley's student governement. In my free time, I enjoy completing word puzzles and practicing brush lettering. Keep scrolling to learn more about some selected projects, my research, and what I'm reading.
